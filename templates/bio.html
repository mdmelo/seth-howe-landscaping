Seth Howe

Professional Landscape Consultant & Certified Arborist

With over 20 years of experience in landscape design, implementation, and maintenance, Seth has established himself as one of the region's most trusted landscape professionals.

Two Decades of Excellence

Professional Journey

Seth's passion for landscaping began in the early 2000s when he recognized the transformative power of well-designed outdoor spaces. Starting as an apprentice with a local landscaping firm, he quickly demonstrated exceptional skill in plant selection, design principles, and client relations.

Over the years, Seth has worked on hundreds of residential and commercial properties, ranging from intimate garden redesigns to large-scale landscape installations. His expertise spans every aspect of landscape consulting, from initial site analysis and design conceptualization to project management and ongoing maintenance planning.

Specialized Expertise

Sustainable Design

Specializes in eco-friendly landscaping solutions that conserve water, support local wildlife, and reduce environmental impact.

Arboriculture

Certified arborist with extensive knowledge in tree health, pruning techniques, and disease management.

Hardscape Integration

Expert in seamlessly blending hardscape elements with natural plantings for cohesive outdoor living spaces.

Problem Solving

Skilled at diagnosing and resolving complex landscape issues including drainage, pest management, and plant diseases.

Philosophy & Approach

Seth believes that every landscape should tell a story - one that reflects the personality and lifestyle of its owners while respecting the natural environment. His design philosophy centers on creating outdoor spaces that are not only beautiful but also functional, sustainable, and low-maintenance.

"I don't just design landscapes; I create outdoor experiences," Seth explains. "Every project begins with understanding how my clients want to use their space, what challenges they face, and what dreams they have for their property."

Community Involvement

Beyond his professional practice, Seth is actively involved in promoting sustainable landscaping practices in the community. He regularly volunteers with local environmental organizations, conducts workshops on native plant gardening, and mentors young professionals entering the landscaping field.

Seth has also contributed to several community beautification projects, donating his time and expertise to enhance public spaces and support local non-profit organizations.
