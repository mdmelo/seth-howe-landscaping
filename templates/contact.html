Frequently Asked Questions

A comprehensive consultation includes site analysis, soil assessment, discussion of your goals and budget, evaluation of existing plants and structures, identification of challenges and opportunities, and preliminary recommendations. The initial consultation is free within our primary service area.

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A design consultation may take 1-2 weeks, while complete landscape designs can take 2-4 weeks. Implementation depends on your contractor and seasonal factors. Seth provides realistic timelines during the consultation phase.

Seth specializes in design and consulting rather than installation. However, he works with a network of trusted contractors and can provide referrals. He also offers oversight services to ensure your project is implemented according to the design specifications.

Seth prioritizes native plants, water conservation, soil health, and ecosystem support in all designs. He creates landscapes that require minimal inputs while providing maximum environmental benefits, including wildlife habitat, stormwater management, and carbon sequestration.
